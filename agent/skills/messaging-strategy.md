# Messaging strategy

Produce a communication strategy before any outreach copy.

Choose:

- `messagingAngle` — the core narrative
- `technicalDepth` — `low` | `medium` | `high`
- `tone` — e.g. peer engineer, concise, non-salesy
- `story` — the account-specific story arc
- `hooks` — opening hooks grounded in research
- `cta` — the ask (demo, architecture chat, specific resource)
- `customerExamples` — relevant patterns from opportunity mapping references
- `likelyObjections` — what they may push back on

If opportunity mapping or hypotheses were skipped, ground the strategy in research alone and keep claims modest.

Persist with `save_stage_output` stage `messaging_strategy`.
