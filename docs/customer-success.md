## Main GTM Themes

- **Scale and traffic resilience:** Weather Company, Ledger, Kiko Milano, Hydrow, and ticket-drop stories emphasize traffic spikes, global scale, and stability under high demand.
- **Developer velocity:** Extra Space Storage, Sitecore, Sonos, Supabase, Wunderman Thompson, and JustInCase repeatedly frame Vercel around faster builds, faster prototyping, and simpler release workflows.
- **AI product shipping:** Flora, Sensay, Stably, OpenEvidence, General Intelligence, Superset, Stripe, and Avalara show Vercel/v0/AI SDK as a way to ship agentic or AI-heavy products quickly.
- **Multi-tenant platforms and custom domains:** Dub, Fern, Potion, mmm.page, and CommerceUI show Vercel as infrastructure for platforms with many domains, tenants, docs sites, or storefronts.
- **Enterprise frontend modernization:** Rippling, Washington Post, PAIGE, Elkjop, Sitecore, Scale, and Wunderman Thompson position Vercel/Next.js as a path to modern composable frontend operations.
